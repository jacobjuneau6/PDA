# The Case
After designing the PCB, I need a case to hold it that battery and the display. I made dummy stand-ins for the battery and display so i could make the assembly and do fit checks. The case design took about 1 hour and while it is not my best work, a quick 3d print of the shape feels fine so i say its good enough. 
I chose to use a more boxy-sharp design simplybecause i think that rounded edges and bevels are not stylish or useful.
